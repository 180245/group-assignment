About us

Trance is a brand that stands for finding peace and quiet. Trance is one of the very first brands that offers a product that focuses on stress relief and stress prevention in the way that we do. With a combined 80 years of experience we offer so much more than just an app, we offer you the feel of relaxation. With a lot of different ways through different features we want to help you get your me-time back.

Our Mission

Trance is a brand that stands for relaxation. Therefore our mission is to help as much people get their me-time back. Whether this is by helping them via our social media or through our app.

Our Vision

“In a world full of busy lives and stressed out people, we believe that there is nothing more important than finding peace and quiet to calm down. Because only when we are relaxed we are our best selves.”
